Poem For My Love


Sweet Chaos

We fight, we clash, we love, we tear, A tangled mess, but we still care.
In every storm, I find your grace, In every battle, I see your face.

You drive me mad, you make me wild, Yet like a child, I crave your smile.
We’re fire and ice, a twisted art, But still, you’re always in my heart.

Through highs and lows, we find our way, Even on days we’ve gone astray.
The love we share, the wars we wage, Are worth it all, page after page.

So here’s my heart, imperfect and true, Forever tangled, just me and you.
