Teddy Day

On Teddy Day, let’s play pretend,
With fuzzy bears, let’s twist and bend.
I’ll hug you close, then kiss you deep,
As secrets stir, we both will keep.

Your soft embrace, my naughty tease,
A cuddle game that’s sure to please.
Teddy’s soft, but my love’s on fire,
Come closer, babe, let’s take it higher.
